About our department

A brief history of department of theatre and media art

The Department of Theatre and Media Art of the Faculty of Arts, Ekiti State University was born in 2014. the idea for the esstablishment of the Department was first muted by Dr.Modupe Olaniyan at the University's Council meeting in 2009 while she was then a member of the Council.

The middlewives are : Professor Rasaki Ojo Bakare and Dr.Modupe Olaniyan, who designed the programme; Professor Mike Abiodun, the Dean, Faculty of Arts, who presented and defend th programme at the senate meting, Professor Patrick Oladipo Aina, the Vice Chancellor who presented the programme to the University's Governing Council and got the Council's approval.

The Vice Chancellor also employed the first staff of the Department, Dr. Gabriel Adekola Oyewo a Reader, on the 24th of Feburary,2014.
